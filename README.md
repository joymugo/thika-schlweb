# Thika Girls Karibaribi 🏫

A modern, responsive, and user-friendly web application built for **Thika School**. This platform serves as a digital hub for students, parents, teachers, and administrators to stay updated with school activities, academics, and announcements.

---

## 🚀 Features

*   **Information Hub:** Comprehensive details about the school's history, mission, vision, and values.
*   **Academics & Department Portal:** Dedicated sections for different subjects, faculty members, and curricula.
*   **Announcements & News Feed:** Real-time updates on school events, term dates, and emergency notices.
*   **Admissions Portal:** Easy-to-use guidance and downloadable forms for prospective students and parents.
*   **Contact & Location:** Integrated contact forms, office hours, and physical address details.
